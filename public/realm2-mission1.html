The Citadel's Shadows

Realm 2: Surveillance City - Mission 1

Introduction

Welcome to the Citadel, a walled city where every transaction is monitored, every purchase is tracked, and financial freedom is just an illusion.

In this city-state, Asha discovers how centralized monetary control creates a surveillance system where privacy becomes a luxury. As she navigates through the Citadel's streets, she begins to understand the implications of centralized digital currencies and the risks they pose to individual freedom.

The Walls Have Eyes

Throughout the Citadel, large screens display citizens' financial status and spending habits. The authorities know exactly who has money, how much, and how they spend it.

When citizens make purchases, their entire financial history becomes available to merchants. The state controls who can and cannot participate in the economy based on their "citizen score."

The Privacy Challenge

As Asha explores further, she notices subtle resistance within the city. Some citizens are finding ways to conduct transactions outside the watchful eyes of the Citadel's financial surveillance system.

To proceed to the next mission, Asha must learn how traditional financial systems track and monitor transactions, and why privacy in financial matters is a fundamental right rather than a suspicious activity.
